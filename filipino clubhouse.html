Filipino Clubhouse

Ingredients
  • 1 Medium Tomatoes, Sliced
  • 6 Slices Cucumber
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 3 large eggs
  • 6 Slices Brown Sliced Bread
  • 2 Slices Cheddar or White Cheese
  • 6 Pieces Romaine Lettuce leaves, washed
  • 2 Pieces Chicken Ham
  • 2-3 Tablespoons Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon vegetable oil
Instructions
  1. Placed the chicken ham in the Pan-Fry Pan till it is cooked (Light brown). Set it aside.
  2. Add 1 to 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil and egg in the same pan to make a scrambled egg. Set it aside when done.
  3. Use the Electric Grill to toast the and browned the bread. Set it aside.
  4. To assembled the sandwich, spread the 1st sliced bread with mayonnaise.
  5. Add ham above the sliced bread, followed by lettuce, scrambled egg and then tomatoes.
  6. Place the 2nd sliced bread with mayonnaise on top of it. Add again lettuce, cheese squares, scrambled egg and then sliced cucumber. Cover it with the 3rd sliced bread.
  7. Place the tooth picked on each side to help hold each other and then divide them into 4 pieces. Serve it with fries or potato chips.
