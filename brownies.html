Brownies

NO OVEN



Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup melted butter (unsalted)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder (unsweetened)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • Chocolate Chips
Instructions
  1. In a large bowl add your butter and sugar, Mix well until completely dissolved.
  2. Add your eggs and vanilla to the bowl.
  3. Sift flour, cocoa powder, and salt and add to the bowl. Then give it a mix and set aside.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a container lined with parchment paper. Flatten and smooth the surface.
  5. Spread the mixture evenly into the container. Top with chocolate chips if desired.
  6. Using a double boiling method, cook on low heat for 30-35 minutes.
  7. Cut the brownies into bite-size pieces and they are ready to serve.
